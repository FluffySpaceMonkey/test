01 / About

Science with a purpose

I grew up curious about how things work — taking apart appliances, asking why the sky is blue, reading about climate change before I really understood it. That curiosity led me to chemical engineering.

I'm not here to build the next flashy app. I want to work on energy storage, carbon capture, and sustainable materials — the unglamorous but essential problems that determine what kind of world we leave behind.

When I'm not in the lab, I'm probably at a cafe with friends, overthinking my coffee brewing method, or exploring the city. I believe the best ideas come when you're not trying to have them.
